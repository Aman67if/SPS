Objectives

Professional Development

To continuously improve journalists' skills through training, workshops, and seminars, ensuring they remain at the forefront of the profession.

Networking and Collaboration

To create opportunities for journalists to collaborate on stories, share knowledge, and build a strong professional network across the state and beyond.

Public Awareness

To educate the public on the role of journalism in democracy, enhancing the public’s understanding and appreciation of ethical reporting.

Health and Welfare

To advocate for journalists' health and welfare, ensuring they have access to medical benefits, insurance, and other necessary support.

Unionizing Journalists

To bring journalists together under a unified body, strengthening their collective bargaining power and ensuring they receive fair treatment.

Awareness of Constitutional Rights

To educate journalists about their constitutional rights, empowering them to defend these rights and report freely and fearlessly.

Creating a Strong Administrative Structure

To develop a robust administrative framework that ensures the smooth functioning of the association, transparency in operations, and accountability to its members.

Bridging the Information Gap

To ensure that accurate and vital information reaches all journalists, particularly those in remote areas, enabling them to report effectively.
